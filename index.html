About Us

Preserving the Filipino Intellect

San Anselmo Publications, Inc. is a Philippine-based independent publisher of books, journals, pamphlets, and audiobooks. We are known for our focus on local literature, arts, and socio-political themes.

We actively participate in book fairs and collaborate with academic institutions to promote critical thinking and cultural appreciation across the nation.
